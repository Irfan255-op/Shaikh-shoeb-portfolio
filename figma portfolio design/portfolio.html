Request

who's handling this

I'm Shoeb Shaikh, a full stack developer who thinks of a product as a single pipeline — a request comes in through the interface, gets reasoned about in the logic layer, and settles somewhere durable in the data layer. I like owning all three, because the gaps between them are usually where bugs and slow pages live.

Day to day that means shipping Bootstrap-driven, responsive front ends, wiring them to Laravel applications with clean routes and services, and designing MySQL schemas that don't fall over once real traffic hits them.
